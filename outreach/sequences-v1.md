# Outreach Sequences v1 — Insurance Agency Owners
**Rules:** <100 words/email. Plain text, no images/links in touch 1–2 (deliverability). One CTA. {first_line} = AI-personalized from agency website/reviews/carrier list. Sender = founder persona, real name. All sends: CAN-SPAM footer w/ address + unsubscribe on later touches; touch 1–2 use reply-to-opt-out language.

**A/B discipline:** 3 angles launch simultaneously, ~1,000 contacts each. Kill anything <1% reply after 500 sends. Winner gets remaining list + new inbox capacity.

---

## Angle A — "Renewal leakage math" (retention $)

**Touch 1 (Day 0):**
Subject: `{agency_name} renewals`

{first_line}

Quick math question: what's one point of retention worth on your book? On a $2M revenue agency, 92% → 94% is real money — and most of the leakage is just quotes and renewals nobody followed up with on time.

We run renewal + follow-up campaigns for independent P&C agencies — done for you, reported in dollars recovered, not opens.

Worth a 15-min look at your renewal calendar? Just reply "show me."

**Touch 2 (Day 3):** Bump. "Any thoughts on the retention math below? Happy to run the numbers on your book size — takes me 2 minutes."

**Touch 3 (Day 7):**
Subject: `re: {agency_name} renewals`
The uncomfortable stat: acquiring a policyholder costs ~5x keeping one, and most agencies' renewal outreach starts 30 days out — when the shopping already happened. Campaigns that start at 90 days are where the lift lives. We set that up and run it. First campaign is $500 flat, 14 days, one segment of your book. If it doesn't produce, we part friends.

**Touch 4 (Day 12):** Breakup. "Closing the loop — if renewal follow-up is already handled at {agency_name}, ignore me. If it's on the someday list, the $500 sprint is the lowest-risk way to test it. Either way, good selling."

## Reply Handling Playbook (AI triage)
- "Interested/show me" → Calendly link + 3-bullet what-happens-next; offer async option (5-min Loom of a sample campaign + report)
- Price question → sprint first ("$500, 14 days, one segment — retainer only if it produces")
- "We use [tool]" → Angle B pivot: "perfect — we operate it, you keep it"
- "Not now" → 60-day nurture tag
- Compliance objection → email-first, consent-attested, state-aware windows; this is a feature
- Opt-out/negative → immediate suppression, polite close, no exceptions

## Case-Study Manufacturing (Day 2 priority)
Offer 2 agencies the sprint FREE in exchange for a testimonial + before/after numbers. Target via Angle B repliers (they already own tools = fastest deployment). This unblocks touch-2 proof lines and the landing page by week 2.
