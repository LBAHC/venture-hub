## Lane 5 — Compliance / "is this even allowed" objection
> Fair question, and honestly the reason agencies should ask it of every vendor. Our campaigns are email-first; SMS only ever carries informational renewal notices with documented consent; cross-sell never goes by text; sending windows respect state rules (Florida's included); opt-outs process in real time with an audit log per client. It's built so your E&O carrier has nothing to say about it.

## Lane 6 — Skeptical / "who are you, no reviews"
> Totally fair — we're new, and we've deliberately made the first step small so nobody has to take a new vendor on faith: $500, 14 days, one segment, results from your own book. If it doesn't produce, it doesn't convert to anything. We'd rather earn the case study than claim one.
