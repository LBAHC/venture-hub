# Idea Backlog — parked, not forgotten
Rule: nothing here gets built before the core model has paying clients. Each entry: idea, why parked, trigger to revisit.

## Tier: "Presence" add-on — review collection + referral engine (Luke, 2026-07-14)
- **What:** productize the review/referral-request automation already in retainer scope as a named add-on tier (or upsell module) — systematic post-interaction review requests, referral asks, response monitoring.
- **Support from our data:** among 211 search-prominent agencies, median 91 Google reviews, 28% over 200 — the visible winners run review collection as a system (solicitation markers found in review text). The invisible long tail is the buyer.
- **Why parked:** crowded feature space (Podium, Birdeye, NiceJob, Levitate's own review tool); our wedge is operation of revenue campaigns — don't blur it pre-revenue.
- **Trigger:** 3+ paying retainers; clients asking "can you also do our reviews"; then price as +$250-500/mo module.

## AI module ladder for the retainer (Luke, 2026-07-14)
Strategy: modules bolt onto the operator retainer (we already have book/stack access + trust) — not standalone SaaS. Stacks clients from $1,000-1,500 toward $2,000-2,500/mo. Sequence by maintenance burden, lowest first:
1. **Renewal Intelligence Pack** (+$250-400/mo): AI pre-renewal brief per policyholder — rate delta, coverage gaps, cross-sell candidates, talking points. Extends fulfillment/book_analysis.py; batch monthly; ~1 hr/client/mo. **Trigger: 3+ retainers.**
2. **Quote-Intake Responder** (+$300-500/mo): instant AI response to web quote requests, info gathering, producer booking. Medium maintenance (~2-3 hrs/client/mo). **Trigger: module 1 stable.**
3. **Service-Desk Triage** (+$400-600/mo): AI-drafted replies to COI/ID-card/billing requests, human-approval gate mandatory (E&O exposure if anything reads as coverage advice — strict scoping). Highest maintenance. **Trigger: month 6+, dedicated capacity.**
4. **AI voice receptionist: permanent no** — most saturated AI offer in the ecosystem (Day-0 research).

## Tier: Website rebuild + AEO (answer-engine optimization) bundle (Luke, 2026-07-14)
- **What:** agency website rebuilds structured for AI-assistant citation (benchmark tables, FAQ schema, quantified content) + ongoing presence ops. Synergy with Luke's agentic-visibility service business.
- **Why parked:** insurance website builds are commoditized (BrightFire, Forge3, Advisor Evolved); project revenue not recurring; distracts from retention wedge. The AEO angle is the only differentiated piece and is unproven as a purchase driver in this niche (see research/content-channels §6).
- **Trigger:** month 3+, with clients as beta sites; or the AEO service proves demand at AHC/Black Spruce first.
