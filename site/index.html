How the sprint works

Fourteen days, one segment of your book, no homework for your staff.

  1. Days 1–2

    Book analysis

    You send a renewal report or book export. We identify the policies renewing in 45–90 days and hand you a one-page Leakage Snapshot: renewals at risk, premium exposed.

  2. Days 3–4
